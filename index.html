Free Tips
Stop losing marks on
these easy fixes
Vocabulary
Stop saying "good" — use Band 8+ words instead
Using "good" repeatedly signals Band 6 to examiners. Replace it with precise vocabulary that shows range.
Beneficial Effective Favourable Positive Valuable
Grammar
Article errors instantly drop your Grammatical Range score
Missing or wrong articles (a/an/the) are the #1 grammar mistake Indian students make. Examiners notice instantly.
First mention → A Already known → THE General → nothing
Writing
Replace "I think" with examiner-friendly phrases
"I think" reads as informal and low-band. Use structured opinion phrases that signal academic writing.
In my view It seems that It is argued that
Linking Words
Stop repeating "and / but / so" — use these instead
Using varied linking words correctly is one of the fastest ways to boost your Coherence & Cohesion score.
However Moreover Consequently For instance
